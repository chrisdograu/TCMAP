Frequently Asked Questions

How can I resolve installation issues?

To resolve installation issues, make sure your device meets the minimum requirements for the game. If the error persists, try reinstalling the game or contact technical support.

My game is crashing, what should I do?

If your game is crashing, try closing other programs that may be consuming too many resources. Also, check if there are any updates available for the game or your operating system.

Where can I find configuration guides?

We provide detailed configuration guides for all devices in our support section. You can consult the guides online or download the PDFs directly from our website.
